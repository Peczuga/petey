PRIVACY POLICY

BANDIT TWEAKING UTILITY SOFTWARE

Effective Date: 2025-07-18

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Bandit tweaking utility software ("Software"), visit our website ("Website"), or purchase our services. By using our Software or Website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

Email Addresses:

  • Collected when you purchase a license
  • Collected when you create an account
  • Collected when you contact us for support
  • Used for license delivery and account management

Hardware Identification (HWID):

  • Automatically collected when the Software is installed and activated
  • Used for license validation and anti-piracy measures
  • Consists of unique hardware identifiers from your computer system
  • Does not include personal files or sensitive system information

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Software, we may automatically collect:

  • Software version information
  • License activation status

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

2.1 Primary Uses

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

Email Addresses:

  • Delivering purchased licenses to your email
  • Sending license activation instructions
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance
  • Sending important updates about the Software
  • Communicating about your account or purchases

Hardware Identification (HWID):

  • Validating legitimate software licenses
  • Preventing unauthorized use and piracy
  • Ensuring compliance with license terms
  • Managing device limits for multi-device licenses

2.2 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:

  • Contract performance (fulfilling license purchases)
  • Legitimate interests (software security and anti-piracy)
  • Consent (where explicitly provided)
  • Legal compliance (where required by law)

3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

3.1 Storage Location and Duration

  • Email addresses: Stored securely in our customer database for the duration of your account relationship
  • HWID data: Stored for license validation purposes and retained for the duration of your license validity

3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Secure servers and database systems
  • Industry-standard security protocols

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

4.1 Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Disclosure

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our legal rights, prevent fraud, or ensure software security
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets (with advance notice to users)

4.3 No Marketing Lists

We do not add your email to marketing lists or share it with advertisers without your explicit consent.

5. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal and contractual obligations)
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request data portability where technically feasible

5.2 Account Management

You can:

  • Contact us to modify or delete your account information

6. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

6.1 Website Cookies

Our Website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Improve user experience and website functionality
  • Provide basic analytics about site usage

6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though some website functionality may be limited if cookies are disabled.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Software and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

If you are located outside our primary jurisdiction, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our servers are located. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. DATA RETENTION

9.1 Retention Periods

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

9.2 Deletion Procedures

When retention periods expire, we securely delete or anonymize your personal information using industry-standard data destruction methods.

10.1 Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or software functionality. We will notify you of material changes by:

11. DATA PROTECTION COMPLIANCE

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including but not limited to GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant privacy legislation where applicable.

12.1 Privacy Inquiries

For questions about this Privacy Policy, to exercise your privacy rights, or to report privacy concerns, please contact us using the information provided at the bottom of this page.

12.2 Response Time

We will respond to privacy-related requests within 30 days of receipt, or as required by applicable law.

12.3 Complaint Resolution
