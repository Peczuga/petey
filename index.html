Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bandit safe to use?

Absolutely! Bandit is designed with safety as the top priority. Every change can be reverted, and we create automatic restore points before major modifications. The tool is regularly scanned for malware and is digitally signed.

Will Bandit void my warranty?

No, Bandit only makes software-level changes that are completely reversible. It doesn't modify any hardware or firmware settings that could affect your warranty. All tweaks are within Microsoft's acceptable use guidelines.

Can I use Bandit on multiple computers?

Yes! Bandit is free for personal use on unlimited devices. For commercial use or deployment in business environments, please contact us for a business license.

Does Bandit work with Windows 11?

Yes! Bandit is fully compatible with Windows 11 and receives regular updates to support new Windows features. It also maintains backward compatibility with Windows 10.

How often is Bandit updated?

We release updates every 2-4 weeks with new features, optimizations, and compatibility improvements. Critical security updates are pushed immediately when needed.

Can I contribute to Bandit's development?

We welcome community contributions! Join our Discord server to connect with the development team, suggest features, report bugs, or contribute to our open-source modules on GitHub.
